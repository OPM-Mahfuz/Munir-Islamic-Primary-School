About Our School

Munir Islamic Primary School provides both Islamic and modern education to nurture future leaders equipped with faith, knowledge, and excellence. Founded with the vision to empower children with strong moral and academic foundations, our school has grown to become a leading institution in the region.

We believe in holistic development, combining quality academics, Islamic values, extracurricular activities, and life skills. Our dedicated teachers ensure every child reaches their full potential in a safe and nurturing environment.

Our Mission

To provide quality Islamic and modern education that develops knowledgeable, responsible, and spiritually grounded individuals.

Our Vision

To be a leading institution recognized for academic excellence, Islamic values, and holistic child development.

Core Values

  • Faith & Spiritual Growth
  • Academic Excellence
  • Integrity & Responsibility
  • Respect & Compassion
  • Leadership & Teamwork

Message from the Principal

“At Munir Islamic Primary School, we are committed to nurturing students who excel academically while upholding strong Islamic values. Our goal is to empower each child with the knowledge, skills, and moral compass to thrive in school and beyond.” – Principal, Munir Islamic Primary School
